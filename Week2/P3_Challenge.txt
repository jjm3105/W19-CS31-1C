// Challenge problem

Given two numbers, representing time in military time, find the difference in
hours and minutes. Assume the second number given is always later than the
first number. The two numbers are not necessarily on the same day, but are
within at most 1 day of each other


Sample Output:
Enter the first time: 1436
Enter the second time: 2322
8 hours and 46 minutes.

Sample Output:
Enter the first time: 2322
Enter the second time: 1436
15 hours and 14 minutes.


Hint:
Use of the / operator and the % operator is critical.


Challenge:
Try this problem without using any if-statements.
