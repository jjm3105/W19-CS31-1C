// Challenge problem

Given two numbers, representing time in military time, find the difference in
time in hours and minutes. Assume the second number given is always later than
the first number. The two numbers are not necessarily on the same day, but are
within at most 1 day of each other


Sample Output:
Enter the first time: 1435
Enter the second time: 2322
8 hours and 47 minutes.

Sample Output:
Enter the first time: 2322
Enter the second time: 1435
15 hours and 13 minutes.


Hint:
Use of the / operator and the % operator is critical.


Challenge:
Try this problem without using if-statements.
